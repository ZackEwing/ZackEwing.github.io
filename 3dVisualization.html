Project Scope

OttoQ Inc. requested 3D visualization to better define parking problems in congested neighborhoods, corporate campuses, and cities during meetings with potential clients and investors.

Contribution

OttoQ was commited to a brand identify that focused on reducing stress and making parking a delightful experience. I developed all of the company's 3D assets in a low-poly art style that reflected their vision of simplicity and delight.
