Project Scope

Osso VR is an award winning VR surgical and medical device training platform. The company is made up of practicing clinicians, professional VR developers and medtech industry veterans. Osso specializes in hyper-realistic, haptic-enhanced, hands-on interactivity and design.

Contribution

I was contracted by Osso VR to produce a series of images that reflected their merger of game design and technology with medical training to be used in presentations and marketing material for the 2017 Department of Education Ed-Sim challenge. The low-poly art style used throughout underlines the union of digital visualization with real world applications.
