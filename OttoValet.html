Project Scope

The OttoValet Valet Management Dashboard was developed in conjunction with the valet management team at Tesla HQ in Palo Alto, CA. It serves as a central command center for coordinating vehicle retrieval, efficiently distributing valet employees, and generating vital analytics collected by drivers using the OttoValet mobile app and Smart QR Tickets.

Contribution

As lead designer on the project I drove all phases of development beginning with on-site visits to the Tesla Palo Alto campus, developing wireframes based on observed use cases, and generating visual assets from mock ups through the final dashboard release.
